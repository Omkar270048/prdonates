Who We Are

Established in 2025, PRDonates is a grassroots non-profit committed to helping the poor by distributing food and used clothing. What began as a small neighborhood initiative has grown into a compassionate movement reaching hundreds of lives each month.

Our dedicated team of volunteers works tirelessly to ensure that no one in our community goes hungry or without proper clothing. We believe in the power of collective action and the difference that small acts of kindness can make in people's lives.
